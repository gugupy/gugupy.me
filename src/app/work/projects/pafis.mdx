## Overview

PAFIS (Predictive AI Fault Isolation System) is a cutting-edge platform designed to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of aircraft maintenance operations. It provides a comprehensive solution for maintenance engineers, enabling them to quickly identify and resolve faults in aircraft systems.

## Technologies Used

- **TailwindCSS & shadcn/ui**: For building a modern, responsive, and beautiful UI.
- **Next.js**: For server-side rendering and static site generation.
- **TypeScript**: For type safety and better developer experience.
